MINION

Targets: Ground And Air Units

Attack Style: single target

Movement Speed: 32

This creature born from another world was able to materialize because of the Dark Elixir. The Minion terrorizes both the land and air in order to bring chaos and destruction. Although its existence in the world is weak, it’s best not to underestimate it.

HOG RIDER

Targets: Ground Units

Attack Style: single target

Movement Speed: 24

The Hog Rider is a unit that has mastered the art of riding leaping pigs. Mind you, mastering the art of riding leaping pigs can do wonders. In fact, because the Hog Rider has mastered this unique skill, no wall can stop it anymore! A world of hurt is the only thing waiting for those who are coward enough to seek protection from walls.

VALKERY

Targets: Ground Units

Attack Style: single target

Movement Speed: 24

The Valkyrie is the mighty wielder of the double axe! It’s unstoppable with its perfectly trained body and brutal axe skills. Foes can’t help but cower in her presence. Units, buildings, everything—nothing is safe in the face of the Valkyrie!

GOLEM

Targets: Ground Units Especially Defenses

Attack Style: single target

Movement Speed: 12

A Golem is a colossal mass of rocks. That being said, a Golem obviously specializes in defense. Its impenetrable skin absorbs attacks of all kinds. And if it so happens that it gets damaged, it will simply split into tinier golems. Such a terrifying beast!

WITCH

Targets: Ground and Air Units

Attack Style: Area Splash

Movement Speed: 12

The Witch never fights alone! Whenever it appears, a massive horde of undead is unleashed as well. The Witch is not only a witch, but it’s also a necromancer! The spirits of the dead are its allies, so beware everyone who tries to oppose the Witch!

LAVA HOUND

Targets: Ground Units Especially Air Defenses

Attack Style: Single Target

Movement Speed: 20

For some reason, a Lava Hound can’t help but target air defenses. No one knows why, but at least there’s something reliable that can fight those pesky air defenses. A Lava Hound has a temper as hot as its flames. It’s so resilient that it won’t even succumb to death without a fight. As a matter of fact, once it is pushed to its limits, it will split into smaller lava pups that can fight just as hard.

ICE GOLEM

Targets: Ground Units

Attack Style: single Target specialy defences

Movement Speed: 12

The Ice Golem is a troop unlocked once the Dark Barracks is upgraded to level 8. Ice Golems are powerful tanking units with low damage but high hitpoints for the space they take. Their attacks slow down whatever they attack, reducing affected Defensive Buildings or units' fire rate by 50% for 2 seconds

HEAD HUNTER

Targets: Ground and Air Units

Attack Style: single Target Especially Heros

Movement Speed: 24

The Headhunter is a troop unlocked once the Dark Barracks is upgraded to level 9, which requires the player to be at Town Hall level 12. Headhunters prioritize Heroes above all other targets, and will bypass all other types of enemy buildings and troops while any enemy Heroes remain on the battlefield.
